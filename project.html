Columbus Beer

Columbus beer is a full stack web application I created alongside a team of five other enginners. The idea behind this project was to create a website where Columbus locals could share their thoughts and experiences trying different beers. With the rise in local brewerys and countless new beers every month, our website alleviated some of the stress from ordering a beer you've never heard of. It also offers a social platform, to comment and tag reviews you agree or disagree with.

Technology Used

Java, Spring, HTML, CSS
